# Excel-Sales-Analytics

This repository contains a set of business intelligence reports built in Microsoft Excel as part of my learning project with AtliQ Hardwares sales data. The reports were created using Power Query, Power Pivot, and advanced Excel features to analyse customer net sales performance and market performance across multiple years.

## Project Overview

The goal of this project was to use Excel to turn raw business data into clear, decision-support reports. The dashboards and reports help answer questions such as:

- How did net sales change from 2019 to 2021?
- Which customers contributed the most to revenue growth?
- How do selected markets perform compared to the full dataset?
- Which markets are underperforming against their 2021 targets?

## Reports Overview
### 1. Customer Net Sales Report
This report shows customer-wise net sales for **2019, 2020, and 2021**, along with **year-over-year growth from 2020 to 2021**. It provides a full customer performance view across all regions, markets, and divisions.

Key highlights:
- Total net sales increased from **87.5M in 2019** to **196.7M in 2020** and **598.9M in 2021**.
- Overall, **2020 vs 2021 growth was 304.5%**.
- Major contributors in 2021 included **Amazon (82.1M)**, **Atliq e Store (53.0M)**, and **AtliQ Exclusive (61.1M)**.

### 2. Customer Net Sales Report – Bangladesh and Australia Filter
This version narrows the analysis to selected markets and compares customer-level performance within that filtered scope.

Key highlights:
- Total net sales for the selected markets rose from **35.2M in 2019** to **62.7M in 2020** and **189.2M in 2021**.
- Overall **2020 vs 2021 growth was 301.6%**.
- Top 2021 customers in the filtered view included **Amazon (27.1M)**, **AtliQ Exclusive (23.5M)**, and **Atliq e Store (13.2M)**.

### 3. Market Performance vs Target Report
This report compares **actual 2021 sales** against **2021 targets** across markets and shows the target gap in both value and percentage terms.

Key highlights:
- Grand total 2021 sales reached **598.9M**, which was **54.9M below target**, or **-9.2% overall**.
- Large markets such as **USA (-10.2M, -11.7%)**, **India (-9.6M, -5.9%)**, and **Canada (-5.1M, -14.5%)** missed target.

## Tools & Skills Demonstrated

- **Microsoft Excel**: Core platform for all reports
- Pivot Tables: Report Design
- Power Pivot: Data modelling and relationship building across tables
- DAX Measures: Custom calculated fields for YoY growth and target variance

- Sales Trend Analysis

The reports are based on the **AtliQ Hardwares** sales dataset, which is commonly used for analytics and reporting purposes. The focus of this project is on transforming business data into meaningful Excel-based reports for decision-making.
